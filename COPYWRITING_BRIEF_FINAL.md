## 9. FAQ SECTION

**Production copy:**

```html
<h2>Questions? We've Got Answers</h2>

<!-- Q1 -->
<h3>How do I see who unfollowed me on Instagram for free?</h3>
<p>Upload your Instagram data ZIP file to UnfollowerTracker. Results appear instantly with everyone who unfollowed you and who doesn't follow back. The first 3 accounts are shown right away. Watch a 30-second video to see your entire list—no payment required.</p>

<!-- Q2 -->
<h3>How do I download my Instagram data ZIP file?</h3>
<p>Open Instagram on mobile or desktop → Settings → Your Activity → Download Your Information. Choose JSON format and confirm your email address. Instagram emails you a ZIP file within a few minutes. Check your spam folder if you don't see it.</p>

<!-- Q3 -->
<h3>Is it safe to upload my Instagram ZIP file here?</h3>
<p>Completely safe. Your file never touches our servers—it's analyzed entirely in your browser. We don't store it, see it, or share it with anyone. You can verify this by disconnecting from the internet after uploading; the results still work.</p>

<!-- Q4 -->
<h3>Why doesn't Instagram show me who unfollowed me directly?</h3>
<p>Instagram intentionally hides this data to reduce drama and conflict on their platform. They want you focused on creating content, not obsessing over who unfollowed you. That's why tools like UnfollowerTracker exist—to give you transparency Instagram won't provide.</p>

<!-- Q5 -->
<h3>Why do I have to watch an ad to see my full unfollower list?</h3>
<p>We're 100% free and completely independent. We don't charge you, ask for your password, or store your data. The 30-second video ad is how we cover our costs and keep the tool alive for everyone. No ads = no free tool.</p>

<!-- Q6 -->
<h3>Does Instagram know I'm using UnfollowerTracker?</h3>
<p>No. UnfollowerTracker never connects to Instagram's servers or accesses your account. We only analyze your downloaded ZIP file, which is publicly accessible data you already own. Using an unfollower tracker doesn't violate Instagram's terms and carries no ban risk.</p>

<!-- Q7 -->
<h3>What's the difference between "doesn't follow back" and "unfollowed me"?</h3>
<p>"Doesn't follow back" means you follow them, but they don't follow you—could be intentional or they never saw your follow request. "Unfollowed me" means they used to follow you but no longer do. UnfollowerTracker shows you both, so you have the full picture.</p>

<!-- Q8 -->
<h3>Does UnfollowerTracker work on iPhone and Android?</h3>
<p>Yes, completely. UnfollowerTracker works on any device with a web browser—iPhone, Android, iPad, desktop, whatever. No app download required. Just visit the site, upload your ZIP, and go.</p>
```

**SEO notes:**
All 8 questions match exact long-tail search queries (conversational intent, ChatGPT/SGE optimization). Answers lead with the direct, actionable response in the first sentence (featured snippet optimization). Coverage includes: how-to (Q1–2), safety (Q3, Q6), why-explanations (Q4–5), definitional/clarification (Q7–8), and device compatibility (Q8). Tone balances authority with friendliness. Q5 honestly frames the ad model; Q6 provides reassurance on ban risk and terms compliance.

## ADDITIONAL IMPLEMENTATION NOTES

### Keyword Integration Summary:
- **Primary keywords:** Naturally incorporated in H1, meta description, testimonials (social proof), and FAQ questions
- **Secondary keywords:** "Ghost followers," "follow back checker," "non-followers," appear in FAQ answers and trust points
- **Long-tail keywords:** Full titles of FAQ questions match exact search queries for SGE/ChatGPT optimization

### Tone Consistency:
- All copy avoids corporate jargon (no "innovative," "cutting-edge," "revolutionize")
- Short sentences (avg 10–15 words) for scanability
- Active voice throughout
- First-person perspective where appropriate ("We don't have servers," "That's how we keep the tool running")

### Trust & Transparency:
- Ad mechanic introduced early in How It Works (Step 3) and reinforced in FAQ (Q5)
- No false claims about scale; uses verified "+2k" claim only
- Privacy claims are specific and verifiable (browser-only processing, no servers)
- FAQs address user objections head-on (ban risk, safety, necessity of ad model)

### SEO Structure:
- Proper heading hierarchy (H1 → H2 → H3)
- FAQ schema-ready formatting
- Internal anchor links (#how-it-works, #why-trust-us, #faq)
- Geographic diversity in testimonials (Austin, London, Toronto) for international reach
